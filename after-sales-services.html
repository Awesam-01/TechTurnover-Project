What does the 12 months warranty include?

7 Days

Within the first 7 days from the date of purchase if the phone experiences a technical defect it will be replaced.

6 Months

Within the first 6 months from the date of purchase if the phone experiences a technical defect the cost of parts and labour are covered for repairing the device.

12 Months

Between the 7th and 12th month from date of purchase if the phone experiences a technical defect the customer will bear the cost of any parts replaced and Badili will cater for the labour cost to replace the part.
